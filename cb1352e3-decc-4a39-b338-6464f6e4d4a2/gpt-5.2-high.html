# Eric m failing class — Secure Centrix81 A fast, replayable **3D school-dungeon RPG** where you play as Eric M., a student trapped in a nightmare version of school. Stress becomes monsters, homework becomes hazards, and **The Teacher** is always hunting you. Built as a single-page web game using **Three.js** (3D) and **Tailwind** (UI). --- ## Quick Start 1. Download/open the project. 2. Open `index.html` in a modern desktop browser (Chrome/Edge/Firefox recommended). 3. Click **Start Run**. No build step required. --- ## Core Gameplay Loop (One More Try) 1. **Explore** a procedurally generated school (maze-like hallways + fixed major rooms). 2. **Earn Credits (0–3)** by dealing with rivals in classrooms. 3. Use the **Library (safe zone)** to recover and spend **Focus** on supplies. 4. After 3 credits: **defeat the Gym boss**. 5. Then push to the **Exam Wing** and face **The Teacher**. 6. Your **ending** depends on Knowledge, detentions, and remaining time. Sessions are designed to be short and replayable with persistent best score. --- ## Controls ### Desktop - **Move:** WASD or Arrow Keys - **Dash/Sprint:** Hold **Shift** (uses stamina) - **Interact:** **E** (Library shop) - **Battle skills:** **1–4** - **Pause:** **Esc** - **Restart run:** **R** ### Mobile / Touch On small screens or coarse pointers, on-screen controls appear: - D-pad for movement - Buttons for Dash / Interact / Confirm --- ## Zones - **Library (Safe Zone):** Teacher cannot enter. Passive HP/Stamina recovery. Contains the Shop. - **Hallways:** Main traversal routes. Random encounters can occur (low chance, cooldown-limited). - **Classrooms:** Rival encounters (Anbu / Saharsh / Calvin). - **Cafeteria:** Higher monster pressure. - **Gym:** Boss fight (Finals Golem). - **Exam Wing:** Final battle (The Teacher). --- ## Teacher Pressure & Detention - The Teacher patrols and pathfinds toward you. - Pressure increases as the Teacher gets close. - If caught outside the Library: - You get sent to **Detention** - Lose time - Often lose an item - The Teacher becomes more dangerous over the run A **Hall Pass** can auto-save you from being caught. --- ## Battles (Turn-Based) When an encounter triggers, the game enters a turn-based battle overlay. ### Player Stats - **HP** (health) - **Stamina** (skills + dash) - **ATK / DEF / SPD** - **Level / XP** (leveling improves stats) ### Skills - **1 — Homework Strike:** solid damage; scales with combo - **2 — Study Shield:** blocks the next hits - **3 — Speed Dash:** adds dodge/initiative style buffs - **4 — Excuse Escape:** attempt to flee (chance depends on SPD + zone) ### Items - **Energy Drink:** restore HP + stamina - **Notebook:** +Knowledge and +XP (instant) - **Study Guide:** +DEF and +Knowledge (instant) - **Pencil:** temporary ATK buff - **Hall Pass:** free escape from chase/battles ### Combo & Score Winning fights increases combo (up to a cap). Higher combo means more score and stronger momentum. --- ## Outsmart Mini-Games Some rivals/bosses can be approached via mini-games instead of direct fighting. - **Reaction timing:** hit Space when the marker is in the success zone. - **Focus memory:** memorize and click the flashing sequence in order. Failing can still lead to battle or consequences, keeping the pace high. --- ## Win / Loss Conditions ### You win by: - Earning **3 credits** - Clearing the **Gym boss** - Reaching the **Exam Wing** and defeating **The Teacher** before time runs out ### You lose if: - **Time reaches 0** before finishing - **HP reaches 0** from pressure/encounters --- ## Endings Endings depend on: - **Knowledge** (from Notebooks/Study Guides) - **Detentions** (penalties) - **Time remaining** Possible outcomes include: - Passed the Class - Barely Passed - Barely Survived - Failed Completely --- ## Tech Notes - **Rendering:** Three.js (WebGL) - **UI:** Tailwind via CDN - **Persistence:** `localStorage` with namespaced keys using `location.pathname` to avoid conflicts - **Map:** Procedural maze + fixed rooms with connectors - **AI:** Grid A* pathfinding for the Teacher (cannot enter Library) --- ## Tips - The **Library is your reset button**: recover, shop, breathe. - Sprinting is powerful, but stamina is life—avoid running empty near the Teacher. - Use **Skip Turn / stamina recovery** in battle when low. - Stack Knowledge early if you want the best ending. --- ## Credits Created for **Secure Centrix81**.
