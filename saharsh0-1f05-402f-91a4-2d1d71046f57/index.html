Released Saharsh 3.0 Mini

Saharsh 3.0 Mini is a 3 billion parameter pretrained and instruction-tuned generative model (text in/text out). Saharsh 3.0 Mini performs significantly better than Llama 3.2 3B.

Saharsh 3.0 Mini is completely free to use without any usage limits and can be accessed here

Saharsh 3.0 Mini scores a 64±11.8% compared to Llama 3.2 3B's 39±11.3% on sample questions from the MMLU benchmark

Saharsh 3.0 Mini is optimized for multilingual dialogue use cases, including agentic retrieval and summarization tasks. Saharsh 3.0 Mini outperforms many of the available open source and closed chat models on common industry benchmarks.

Model Architecture: Saharsh 3.0 Mini is an auto-regressive language model that uses an optimized transformer architecture. Saharsh 3.0 Mini uses supervised fine-tuning (SFT) and reinforcement learning with human feedback (RLHF) to align with human preferences for helpfulness and safety.

English, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Spanish, and Thai are officially supported. Saharsh 3.0 Mini has been trained on a broader collection of languages than these 8 supported languages.

Instead of relying solely on pretrained knowledge, Saharsh 3.0 Mini can navigate Secure Centrix81, execute functions and perform complex operations.
